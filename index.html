About Us

Our Story

Maranatha Home was established on September 12, 2012. We began with 18 students on our first day, but the school rapidly grew due to the innovative approaches we introduced to the community, which set us apart from existing schools at the time. Initially, we offered classes from Nursery to Grade 6. In 2016, we started administering the BECE, and from 2016 to 2018, our students consistently achieved outstanding results, with our lowest grade being Aggregate 10, which enabled us to top the district for three consecutive years. Today, we proudly serve over 350 students.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a supportive and inclusive learning environment that inspires students to achieve their full potential, think critically, and develop into compassionate, responsible, and innovative individuals, prepared to succeed in an ever-changing world.

Our Vision

To empower children with knowledge, skills, and values, fostering academic excellence, creativity, and character development, while nurturing a community of lifelong learners who will make a positive impact in the world.
